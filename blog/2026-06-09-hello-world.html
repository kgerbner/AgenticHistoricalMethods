Hello, World: Starting an Agentic History Blog

metaintro

Welcome to the workbench! 🌸

This is where I'll share experiments in using Claude as a research assistant for history — the wins, the dead ends, and the verification habits that keep it honest.

What to expect

Short, practical posts: a transcription I attempted, a prompt that worked, a translation that almost fooled me. Each one ties back to the principle that AI drafts and the historian decides.

Why bother?

Because these tools are already in our students' hands and our colleagues' workflows. Better to learn their craft openly — with citation discipline and archival care — than to pretend they aren't there.
