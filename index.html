The idea

Research support, not research replacement

Claude won't write your monograph or decide what matters — but it can transcribe a tricky hand, surface patterns across hundreds of sources, draft a literature review you'll then rip apart, and rubber-duck your half-formed arguments at midnight. This site is about doing that well: with verification, citation discipline, and a clear sense of the craft's ethics.
